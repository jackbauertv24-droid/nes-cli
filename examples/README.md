## Important Notes

**MMC3 Mapper Limitations:**
- SMB3 uses CHR bank switching - tile numbers change meaning during gameplay
- Tile 177 at frame 1800 ≠ Tile 177 at frame 300
- Sprite extraction works by reading framebuffer pixels directly (not CHR ROM)

**Screen Edge Clipping:**
- SMB3 title screen has characters entering from edges
- Luigi at x=0-8 is cut off on left
- Mario at x=240-248 is cut off on right
- Best extractions come from frames when characters are in center

**NES Palette Design:**
- Palette[0] = black (transparent)
- Palette[3] = black (sprite outline)
- Both are RGB(0,0,0) - can't distinguish by color matching
- Sprite extractions keep all pixels including black outline
